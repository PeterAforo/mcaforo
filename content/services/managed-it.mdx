# Managed IT Support

Focus on your business while we handle your technology. Our managed IT services provide reliable, proactive support to keep your systems running smoothly.

Reliable, secure hosting for your websites and applications:
- 99.9% uptime guarantee
- SSL certificates included
- Daily backups
- CDN integration
- Performance optimization

### Domain Management
Complete domain lifecycle management:
- Domain registration and renewal
- DNS configuration
- Email setup (MX records)

### Website Maintenance
Keep your website secure and up-to-date:
- Security updates and patches
- Plugin/dependency updates
- Performance monitoring
- Content updates
- Bug fixes

### 24/7 Monitoring
Proactive monitoring to catch issues before they impact your business:
- Uptime monitoring
- Performance alerts
- Security scanning

- Backup verification

### Help Desk Support
Responsive support when you need it:
- Email and ticket support
- Phone support (Growth & Enterprise)
- Remote troubleshooting
- On-site support (Enterprise)
