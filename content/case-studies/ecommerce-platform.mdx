# E-commerce Platform Redesign

## The Challenge

RetailGH, a growing retail business in Accra, was struggling with an outdated e-commerce platform. Their existing site was slow, difficult to navigate, and not mobile-friendly. This was causing high cart abandonment rates and lost sales.

## Our Solution

We designed and built a completely new e-commerce platform using modern technologies:

- **Next.js** for fast, SEO-friendly pages
- **Headless CMS** for easy product management
- **Flutterwave** integration for seamless payments
- **Mobile-first design** for the majority of their users

## Key Features

### Improved User Experience
- Intuitive navigation and search
- Quick checkout process
- Mobile-optimized design

### Performance Optimization
- Server-side rendering for fast initial loads
- Image optimization and lazy loading
- CDN for global content delivery

### Business Tools
- Real-time inventory management
- Automated order notifications
- Customer analytics dashboard

## Results

After launching the new platform:

- **150%** increase in online sales within 3 months
- **40%** reduction in cart abandonment
- **3x** faster page load times
- **60%** of orders now come from mobile devices
